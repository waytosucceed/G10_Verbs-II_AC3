Proverbs with the word “Grow”

“Growing old is inevitable, growing up is optional”: Everyone will age physically — that’s unavoidable. But maturity, responsibility, and emotional growth are choices. You can get older without necessarily “growing up.”
Example: Although he turned 40, his playful attitude showed that growing old is inevitable, but growing up is optional.

“Grass doesn't grow on a busy street”: Constant activity prevents growth or development. In life, if someone is always rushing or restless, deeper progress or stability may not take root.
Example: Her career was full of constant travel and change, proving that grass doesn’t grow on a busy street.
